BCx by BasiCare PLUS - HEALTHCARE SERVICES WEB APP

The challenge was that not all healthcare problems are an emergency, so why should you have to wait a half hour in a doctors office to get a simple question answered. Wouldn't it be more convienient for the patient to get a simple answer from the comfort of their own home? TRIUMPHANT LIFE CHURCH - LANDING PAGES & MEDIA LIBRARY

Project's goal was multi-fold. The primary goal came from questions coming into the organization coming through phone and email: it was discovered that people mostly wondered what times were. I led the UI redesign in making the info about service times easy to locate. The church also had more than one physical location, so making it easy for visitors to find the locations and service time was a primary goal.
Another site's goal was to serve as a library of audio for people to discover before visiting the place in person. Wouldn't it be best to get a preview of what type of organization it is before visiting?
Thirdly, a goal was making it clear and easy to navigate to so that one can donate to the cause if they feel so obligated. The organization provides support for those in need in Africa.
