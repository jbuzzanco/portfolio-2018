Hello, I'm Joel 

I am a UI/UX Designer with a background in Frontend Development.


Intuitively, I seek what works and what doesn't in user experience design. I am fascinated by design that delights and drives action. My background in development allows me as a designer to communicate fluently with cross-functional teams.

I went to school in Boston to study Web Development at General Assembly. Since graduating, I have worked with some wonderful people: from non-profits, to innovative startups designing products and landing pages.

I have an instinct in understanding data for good UX, to drawing wireframes, to communicating with developers for production. You can read more about me here.

The fact is, there is a lot of badly designed software in the world. I am meticulous in the process of curating welcoming design to products that can be realistically developed and brought into the world – that blend form & function seamlessly together.
